How The Catchup Trader Works

  • Australia exports commodities - AUD tends to follow gold prices
  • The edge: When gold moves but AUD doesn't follow YET, trade the catchup
  • Example: Gold up 2% but AUD only up 0.5% → Buy AUD (will catch up)
  • Opposite: Gold down 2% but AUD only down 0.5% → Sell AUD (will catch down)
  • Why it works: Most people trade AFTER things move together. This is BEFORE.
  • Unconventional edge: Exploits temporary disconnections between correlated markets

Real Example

Scenario: Gold rallies +3% over 5 days (mining strike in South Africa)

AUD reaction: Only +0.8% (lagging)

Trade: BUY AUD - it will catch up to gold's move

Result: AUD rises another +1.5% over next week as markets align
