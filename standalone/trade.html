Getting Started with FX Trading

  • What is AUD/USD?How many US cents 1 AUD buys
  • AUD/USD = 0.7122 means$1 AUD = $0.7122 USD
  • LONG (Buy) meansYou think AUD will go UP
  • SHORT (Sell) meansYou think AUD will go DOWN
  • FLAT meansNo clear signal - don't trade
  • A "pip" is0.0001 move (e.g. 0.7100 to 0.7101)

The Chameleon Strategy

  • IdeaDifferent markets need different strategies
  • In calm marketsFollow the trend (momentum)
  • In crazy marketsBuy dips, sell rallies (mean revert)
  • Why unconventionalMost people stick to one strategy always
  • Historical return+14.91% over 14 months

The Catchup Trader Strategy

  • IdeaAUD follows gold (Australia exports it)
  • The edgeTrade BEFORE AUD catches up to gold's move
  • ExampleGold up 3%, AUD only up 0.5% = Buy AUD
  • Why unconventionalMost people trade AFTER alignment, not before
  • Historical return+13.26% over 14 months

Live quote check

Fetches today's reference rate from Frankfurter (public API). Compare to the AUD/USD shown on Signals. Small differences are normal (daily close vs "latest"). A large gap usually means your bundle is stale — refresh data and redeploy.
